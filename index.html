Places Must Visit

elephant falls

I had visited it in October 14

The original Khasi name for the falls is Ka Kshaid Lai Pateng Khohsiew, which translates to "the three - step waterfall".This name is still used locally. However the modern name is explained by a signboard near the waterfall.The name originated in the British era when the Englishmen spotted a giant rock that looked like an elephant near the fall. This rock was destroyed in the 1897 due to an earthquake.

Review on movies

The Multiverse
great film to watch with family

I really liked this movie idk what all the bad reviews are talking about. This movie is mysterious and a little sci-fi. The end could’ve been better instead of just bringing it back to the beginning. It feels like the writers were out of time and just winged the ending. Even though the ending was kinda disappointing, I liked how they tied the ending and the beginning together in a loop which kinda goes with the plot of the movie. I would totally recommend this movie especially if you like crazy/mysterious theories about the world and the universe. I’d give this a 9/10. Don’t listen to the low reviews y’all cause this movie ain’t bad.

Angry Birds 2
great film to watch with family

The Angry Birds Movie 2 proved to be a fast-paced, funny (outright hilarious in many scenes), and enjoyable film. The story-line may be simple, but one can only stretch a premise borne out of a game app so much. But it is cohesive story-telling, which propels the CGI animation splendidly without suffering through contrived dialogue, specifically dialogue peppered with so much trendy slang in an attempt by many movie companies to broaden the target audience to include young adults - that demographic who'd be "too cool" to be caught dead watching a movie that's supposedly aimed at younger kids.
